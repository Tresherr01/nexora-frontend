Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

NEXORA ("we", "our", "us") is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains what we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

01 — What We Collect

When you use NEXORA, we may collect:

  • Account data — name, email address, password (hashed)
  • Usage data — pages visited, features used, AI queries made
  • Payment data — handled entirely by Lemon Squeezy; we never store card details
  • Uploaded data — CSV/spreadsheet files you upload for analysis
  • Technical data — IP address, browser type, device info, cookies

02 — How We Use Your Data

  • To provide and improve the NEXORA platform
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions
  • To send product updates and important notices (you can opt out)
  • To analyze usage and fix bugs
  • To comply with legal obligations

03 — Your Uploaded Data

Files you upload for AI analysis are stored securely and used solely to power your analytics session. We do not sell, share, or train AI models on your business data. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.

04 — Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share data only with:

  • Lemon Squeezy — payment processing
  • Anthropic — AI analysis (your queries are processed per their privacy policy)
  • Render / Vercel — infrastructure hosting
  • Law enforcement when required by law

05 — Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication (JWT tokens stored in localStorage) and anonymous analytics. We do not use advertising cookies.

06 — Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. Uploaded files are retained for 30 days after upload, then deleted automatically. You can request immediate deletion by contacting us.

07 — Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EU/EEA/UK, you have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Export your data in a portable format
  • Object to processing

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@nexora.ai

08 — Security

We use HTTPS encryption, hashed passwords (bcrypt), and JWT-based authentication. We regularly review our security practices. No system is 100% secure — if you discover a vulnerability, please report it responsibly.

09 — Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy as we grow. We'll notify registered users of significant changes via email. Continued use of NEXORA after changes constitutes acceptance.

10 — Contact

Questions? Reach us at privacy@nexora.ai
